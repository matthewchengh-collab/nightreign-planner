What we need from Bandai (the asks)

Ask 1 — Approve a year-long discount ladder. Standard up to ~33% on deep Steam sales (Winter, Summer), Deluxe up to ~50% (it's 10× more discount-elastic — proven in Dec data). One approval, not twelve.

Ask 2 — Land a new Nightreign DLC drop in this window. Forsaken Hollows alone delivered ~12% of FY26 from a half-year run. A fresh drop around Sep would re-anchor the whole catalog.

Ask 3 — Co-market the Switch 2 launch into PC keys. The Aug 28 Tarnished Edition is a giant awareness moment. We want assets/timing to convert that attention into discounted PC catalog keys in CN/HK/TW.
