Which months sold most in FY26 (Apr 2025 – Mar 2026), and why.

New release / DLC Steam sale CN/Asia festival or shopping event Other
The pattern: almost every spike is a Steam discount sale, usually stacked on top of a Chinese shopping festival. The two exceptions are the May 2025 Nightreign launch (new release, full price) and Dec 2025, the biggest month, where Steam Winter + Double 12 + holiday gifting all land together. Pure cultural festivals with no discount mechanic (Gamescom, Mid-Autumn, anniversary) barely move units — discount is the real driver.

Two independent years of monthly data. The question that matters: do the seasonal spikes actually repeat, or was FY26 a one-off?

Answer: yes, the pattern holds. Across FY25 and FY26, December (Winter Sale) and March (Spring Sale) are top-2 months both years. August is the dead zone both years (FY25's single lowest). November / Double 11 underperforms both years. The plan is built on a repeating pattern, not a single year's luck.
