The opening: Steam runs ~5 sales a year, deliberately spaced months apart. The base game only rode 3 — Summer, Winter, Spring. It sat out Steam Autumn (November) and Steam Lunar New Year (February) at full price, redeeming 0 both times. Those two skipped sales are already-existing, naturally-spaced redemption windows — November sits between Summer and Winter, February between Winter and Spring. Turning them on means going from 3 draw-down events a year to 5, with no double-discount risk and no new mechanic to invent.
